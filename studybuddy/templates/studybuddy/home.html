Welcome to StudyBuddy!


A place to find people to study and make friends with

Sometimes reaching out to classmates to study together is hard, well thanks to StudyBuddy we've made it easier to find other people in your class to form a study session with. It could be someone you really wanted to talk to but haven't had the opportunity to do so yet or it could be friends of yours that you want to formally create a study group with and let others in your class join! Here at StudyBuddy we want to reduce the steps it takes to study with one another, so you can just focus on that exam, presentation or group assignment of yours!

New Users

Profile

First thing you want to do as a new user of StudyBuddy is create your profile! After building your profile start adding classes to your schedule to create study posts and view study sessions for that particular class!

Create your profile

Planner

Planner is where you will see all of your finalized study sessions that either you created and finalized or you're a part of a finalized study session. Both instances you will see the specific day, time and location of the session. As a new user you will not have any finalized study session so to do so start creating or join study posts!

Study Posts

Study Posts contains all the study sessions that other StudyBuddy users created and are not finalized yet. Here you can sort all sessions from when they were created, latest and earliest date/time and class name. You can join a study session that interests you and wait for the creator of the session to accept your request to join. You can also filter sessions by your friends list, ones you created and ones you are attending.

Chat

Chat provides StudyBuddy users with the opportunity to message others through chat rooms that are available or through new ones that you can create! Message others in your study sessions or message people that you want advice from on a certain class!
